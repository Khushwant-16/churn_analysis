# 📊 Customer Lifecycle Analytics & Churn Prediction

> An end-to-end **data analytics pipeline** designed to optimize **customer retention, segmentation, and sales strategy**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project transforms raw transactional data from the **Online Retail II dataset** into **actionable business intelligence**.  
By engineering behavioral features and applying machine learning techniques, the system segments customers, predicts churn risk, and uncovers cross-selling opportunities using **association rule mining**.

This project demonstrates proficiency in **Data Engineering, Statistical Analysis, and Predictive Modeling**, aligning with industry standards for **Data & Analytics roles**.

## 🚀 Key Features & Methodology

### 1️⃣ Data Engineering & Cleaning
- Performed **ETL processing** on a high-volume transactional dataset containing **1M+ records**.
- Implemented robust data cleaning procedures:
  - Handled **negative quantities** (returns/cancellations).
  - Managed **missing values**.
- Ensured **high data reliability** for downstream analysis.

---

### 2️⃣ Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)

- Engineered **Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM)** metrics to quantify customer behavior.
- Applied **K-Means Clustering** to group customers into meaningful segments such as:

| Segment | Description |
|------|------|
| 🏆 Champions | Highly active and high-spending customers |
| 💎 Loyal Customers | Consistent repeat buyers |
| ⚠️ At Risk | Previously active customers showing decline |

This segmentation enables **data-driven marketing strategies**.

---

### 3️⃣ Predictive Churn Analysis

- Developed a **churn detection algorithm** to identify customers likely to stop purchasing.
- Focused on providing **predictive insights** for proactive business decisions.
- Enables **retention marketing campaigns** targeting vulnerable segments.

---

### 4️⃣ Market Basket Analysis

- Applied the **Apriori Algorithm** to identify **frequent itemsets**.
- Generated **association rules** to discover relationships between products.
- Enables:
  - Cross-selling opportunities
  - Product bundling strategies
  - Personalized recommendations

---

## 📊 Business Impact

### 🎯 Targeted Marketing
- Identified **high-value customer segments** for loyalty programs and targeted campaigns.

### 🔄 Churn Mitigation
- Built a framework to detect **"At Risk" customers** early and intervene before churn occurs.

### 📦 Inventory Optimization
- Used **association rules** to recommend **product bundles** and improve product placement strategies.
